About the Conference

Connect. Innovate. Collaborate.

The Thirty-Sixth KKHTCNN Symposium on Civil Engineering will be held at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology from 2 to 5 December 2025. This annual gathering brings together faculty and students from seven leading Asian universities, renowned for their excellence in civil engineering, to share the latest developments and emerging directions in civil engineering.

Participants will present cutting-edge research findings, exchange innovative ideas, and explore new collaborative opportunities to advance the field.

Conference Venue

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)

The 36th KKHTCNN Symposium on Civil Engineering will be held at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, a world-class institution renowned for its excellence in engineering and technology education and research.

Located in the beautiful Clear Water Bay area, HKUST offers state-of-the-art academic facilities, stunning campus views, and a conducive environment for academic discourse and collaboration.

Venue Details

  • Main Venue: Lecture Theater A, Academic Building
  • Technical Sessions: Various rooms in Academic Building
  • Welcome Dinner: LG7 Kitchen 2, HKUST

Campus Navigation Tips

Elevator Navigation System

The Academic Building has over 30 elevators with a systematic numbering system. To find your destination, locate the elevator nearest to your desired location.

Elevator numbers follow a logical sequence that corresponds to building sections, making navigation easier once you understand the pattern.

HKUST Path Advisor

For real-time navigation assistance on campus, we recommend using the official HKUST Path Advisor app. This helpful tool provides step-by-step directions to any location on campus.
