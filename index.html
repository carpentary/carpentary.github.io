About the Conference

Connect. Innovate. Collaborate.

The 36th KKHTCNN Symposium on Civil Engineering is a premier international event bringing together leading experts, researchers, and professionals in the field of civil engineering. This year, we are honored to host the symposium in the vibrant city of Hong Kong, with the theme "Sustainable Infrastructure for Future Cities".

The conference will cover cutting-edge topics including structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, construction management, sustainable materials, and smart infrastructure. Through keynote speeches, technical sessions, workshops, and networking events, we provide participants with opportunities for knowledge exchange and collaboration.

Whether you're an academic researcher, industry professional, or engineering student, the 36th KKHTCNN Symposium will offer valuable insights, connections, and inspiration for your work and career.

Conference Venue

Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre

The 36th KKHTCNN Symposium on Civil Engineering will be held at the prestigious Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, one of Asia's most iconic and well-equipped event venues.

Located in the heart of Hong Kong's vibrant business district, the HKCEC offers state-of-the-art facilities, excellent transport connections, and breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour.

Venue Details

  • Address: 1 Expo Drive, Wan Chai, Hong Kong
  • Capacity: Up to 5,000 attendees
  • Facilities: Multiple conference halls, exhibition areas, breakout rooms, and business centers
  • Transportation: Easily accessible via MTR, bus, taxi, and ferry services
  • Accommodation: Numerous hotels within walking distance
